Help Center

User Guide

Your simple, step-by-step manual to automate your recruiting tasks and market research without any technical skills.

Welcome to your new HR Assistant

This platform acts as your virtual recruiter. It can find candidates, generate highly-personalized messages using AI, and scan the job market for you while you focus on other work. Your local agent safely processes everything in the background.

How to use the Tools

Tool A: AI Sourcing & Outreach

What it does: Finds people based on a Job Title, filters the best matches, and lets you send them messages automatically.

Two ways to send messages:

  • Batch Mode: Check multiple candidates, edit the "Default Batch Message" box, and click Send. The bot will send the same text to everyone.
  • AI Sniper Mode: Click the AI Draft button next to a specific candidate. The system will read their real LinkedIn profile in the background and instantly generate a single, highly-personalized message tailored exclusively for them.

Tool B: Custom List (Manual)

What it does: Sends messages to a specific list of people you already have in a Google Sheet.

How to use it: Paste your Google Sheet link, write your message, and hit Start.

Important: Your Google Sheet must be set to "Anyone with the link can view". The first row must contain columns named exactly Name and LinkedIn URL.

Tool C: Find Open Roles

What it does: Scans job boards across the internet to find companies actively hiring for a specific role.

How to use it: Enter the role and location. The system will search the live market and automatically create a clean Excel Report directly in your connected HR Google Drive folder.

Pro Tip (Target Companies): Add your competitor or dream companies to the Favorites list above the search bar. If the bot finds open roles at these specific companies, they will be highlighted with a gold star and pushed to the top of your results!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Why does the system pause between invites?

To keep your account 100% safe. LinkedIn monitors for robotic behavior. By waiting 8 to 14 seconds between actions, the system mimics a real human clicking and typing.

How does the daily limit work?

To protect your LinkedIn from restrictions, the tool limits you to 15 invites per day. You can see your progress in the bottom left corner. It resets every day.

What does "{name}" do in messages?

It is a smart tag. If you write "Hi {name}", the bot will automatically replace it with the candidate's actual first name before sending the message, making it personal.

How does AI Sniper Mode work?

When you use AI Sniper, the bot reads the candidate's real LinkedIn profile in the background. It analyzes their skills and recent experience to generate a highly personalized, natural-sounding invitation that dramatically increases acceptance rates.

Where are my candidate and job reports saved?

To keep your team aligned, the system bypasses local downloads. Whenever a search finishes, it automatically generates a clean Excel report and uploads it directly to your connected HR Google Drive folder.
